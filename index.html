Lean startup MVP - hypothesis test

Smarter packaging for every online store.

Run one real-SKU audit. Compare transparent packaging options, estimate an illustrative cost range and request a supplier quote. The MVP is designed to test customer behavior and willingness to pay before we build expensive integrations.
